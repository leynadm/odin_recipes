Oatmeal

Description

This is a delicious and healthy recipe for oatmeal.

Oatmeal is a super healthy choice for food, due to its high nutritional value.

Ingredients

  • 250 g of oatmeal
  • 500 ml of milk
  • 50 g of peanut butter
  • 1 banana
  • 5-8 g of cinnamon
  • 1 yogurt
  • Any fruit topping you'd like (strawberries, mandarins, etc.

Steps to follow

  1. Take a big bowl where to prepare everything
  2. Add the oatmeal
  3. Add the milk
  4. Microwave for 5 minutes
  5. Add the peanut butter and stir until the peanut butter and the oatmeal is mixed
  6. Cut a banana in small pieces, add it to the oatmeal and stir some more
  7. Add the cinnamon and stir once again
  8. Add the yogurt on top and still once again
  9. Add the topping (no more stirring is needed)
