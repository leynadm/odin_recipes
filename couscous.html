Couscous

Description

This is a delicious, healthy and dense-calorie recipe for couscous.

Couscous is a super healthy choice for food, due to its high nutritional value.

Ingredients

  • 225 g of couscous
  • 400 ml of water
  • 1 can of sardines
  • 1 can of mussels
  • 3 eggs
  • 150 g of lentils
  • 50 g of olives
  • 100 g of cherry tomatoes
  • Plenty of herbs you like (salt, pepper, curry, oregano, paprika etc.)

Steps to follow

  1. Add the 250 g of couscous in a bowl and add the spices you like
  2. Boil the 400 ml of water
  3. Add the water on top of the couscous and cover it with a lid. Let it rest for the next 5-10 minutes
  4. Prepare to fry the eggs (as you'd normally fry the eggs)
  5. Take a plate and put on it the lentils, olives and mussels
  6. By now the eggs should be fried, so add them on the plate as well
  7. Take the lid off of the oatmeal bowl and stir it a bit
  8. Take the can of sardines, add it on top of the oatmeal (with the oil included) and then stir it all together

At this point you can enjoy your healthy ~1500 kcal meal!
